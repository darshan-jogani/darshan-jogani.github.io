## Darshan Jogani
### Research Engineer | Electrochemical Energy Systems | Techno-Economic Analysis

I develop and evaluate next-generation hydrogen production systems with a focus on alkaline water electrolysis, process optimization, and the economic viability of sustainable fuel pathways.

My work sits at the intersection of electrochemistry, process systems engineering, and energy system integration — helping bridge the gap between laboratory innovation and industrial deployment.
